# Code of Conduct

HexGuard expects contributors and maintainers to keep the project technically rigorous and
respectful.

## Expected behavior

- communicate directly without personal attacks
- assume good intent while challenging weak technical reasoning
- document tradeoffs and constraints clearly
- be respectful of different backgrounds, identities, and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- sexualized language or unwanted attention
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- sustained disruption of technical discussion or moderation

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, discussions, or pull requests that make
the project unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

Report conduct problems to `maintainers@hexguard.dev`. Include links, timestamps, and enough
context for maintainers to investigate.
